Before We Meet

A few questions to make the day yours.

10–12 minutes. No wrong answers. Your responses shape the agenda — the more candid, the more useful.

Strategy & Priorities
What matters most to Arkansas right now — in your own words.
1

What is the single most important thing you want to walk out of this workshop having decided or aligned on?

A decision, a direction, a commitment — whatever would make the day feel worthwhile.

2

What are your top 2–3 strategic priorities for the next 12 months?

Funded initiatives, active programs, or decisions that need to be made soon.

3

Where is the biggest opportunity for AI or automation to change how your team works?

Describe the pain point or opportunity — don't worry about whether SAP supports it.

4

Have you experimented with AI tools — inside or outside SAP — and what happened?

Any pilot, informal experiment, or use of tools like Copilot, ChatGPT, or SAP Joule counts.

Ariba · Concur · Fieldglass
# Does this sound familiar? Applies? Notes
1 Employees manually enter and itemize expenses — receipt capture and auto-population would save significant time
