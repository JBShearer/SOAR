Strategy & Priorities
Help us understand where Arkansas is heading and what success looks like for your team.
1

What is the single most important thing you want to walk out of this workshop having decided or aligned on?

This could be a decision, a direction, a commitment, or a shared understanding.

2

What are the top 2–3 strategic priorities for your agency or function over the next 12 months?

Think about initiatives that are funded, in-flight, or actively being planned.

3

Where do you see the biggest opportunity for AI or automation to change how your team works?

Don't worry about whether SAP supports it — describe the problem or opportunity in your own words.

4

Is there anything about your current SAP environment that you feel is holding back progress?

Known gaps, pain points, frustrations with existing tools or processes.

5

Have you seen an AI or automation use case — inside or outside of SAP — that made you think "we should do something like that"?

From a conference, another agency, a vendor demo — anything that sparked an idea.

6

Do you currently use any SAP or non-SAP AI tools? What has your experience been?

Even informal experiments or pilots count. What worked, what didn't, what surprised you?
